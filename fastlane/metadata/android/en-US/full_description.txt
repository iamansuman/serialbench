SerialBench is a general-purpose serial communication tool for Android,
built for makers, embedded developers, and hobbyists working with
USB-connected microcontrollers and devices.

Connect over USB OTG to any serial device - Arduino, ESP8266, ESP32,
and other microcontrollers - and send or receive data directly from
your Android device. Whether you're debugging firmware, testing a
communication protocol, or just watching raw output from a board,
SerialBench gives you a straightforward way to talk to your hardware
on the go.

Features:

* USB OTG serial connection to a wide range of devices
* Send and receive data over serial
* Live serial terminal for raw text output
* Live plotter view for visualizing numeric data in real time
* Configurable baud rate and connection settings
* Simple, no-frills interface built for development workflows
* No cloud services, no tracking - everything runs on-device

SerialBench is meant to be a pocket-sized alternative to a desktop
serial terminal, useful anywhere you need to interact with a
microcontroller without carrying a laptop.
